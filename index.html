our mission

The Love Fridge is a mutual aid network that sets up community-run, free-food fridges across the city of Chicago. Our community refrigerators are hosted by local businesses and supported through a network of more than 50 organizers across Chicago. Our goal is to empower communities, feed people and eliminate food waste.

what is mutual aid?

Mutual aid is not a form of charity. Mutual aid is a guiding principle for practicing reciprocal and collective care. Mutual aid networks provide a way to meet the basic needs of the community while allowing each other to contribute what we have to the common good.

want to get involved?

There's a bunch of different ways to get involved. Have a fridge that you don't need anymore? Donate it. Have an accessible location that could be home to a beautiful community fridge? Host one. Want to help keep the fridges fully stocked and clean? Volunteer.
